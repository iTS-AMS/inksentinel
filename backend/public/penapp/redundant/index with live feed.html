Proctopen

Bluetooth

  1. Press mode button on ESP32 until OLED shows [Bluetooth].
  2. Click Scan & Connect and select Proctopen.

WiFi — Access Point

  1. Press mode button until OLED shows [WiFi AP].
  2. Join WiFi Proctopen (password: proctopen123).

WiFi — Station

  1. Press mode button until OLED shows [WiFi STA].
  2. Connect this device to the same router.

USB Serial

  1. Press mode button until OLED shows [USB Serial].
  2. Connect ESP32 via USB cable.
  3. Click Connect — pick ESP32 port from browser popup.
Requires Chrome or Edge. Firefox/Safari not supported.
