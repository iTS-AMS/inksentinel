Proctopen

Bluetooth

  1. Press the mode button on the ESP32 until the OLED shows [Bluetooth].
  2. The ESP32's Proctopen WiFi hotspot will still be running for the Wemos units — you do not need to do anything for that.
  3. Click Scan & Connect below. Your browser will show a Bluetooth device picker — select Proctopen.
Web Bluetooth requires Chrome or Edge. It does not work on Firefox or Safari.

WiFi — Access Point

  1. Press the mode button on the ESP32 until the OLED shows [WiFi AP].
  2. On this device, connect to the WiFi network named Proctopen with password proctopen123.
  3. Once connected, click Connect below.
The Wemos units will also connect to Proctopen automatically. No extra configuration needed.

WiFi — Station

  1. Press the mode button on the ESP32 until the OLED shows [WiFi STA].
  2. First time? The ESP32 opens a setup portal. Join Proctopen-Setup WiFi and visit 192.168.4.1 to enter your router credentials.
  3. The ESP32 will reboot and join your router. Connect this device to the same router network.
  4. The Wemos units will receive the router credentials automatically and switch networks on their own.
  5. Click Connect below.
The ESP32's Proctopen hotspot stays running even in STA mode. Wemos units always have a fallback connection path.
